Rules and Regulations

  1. No illegal items should be sold on the platform, for example, marijuana, animal skins, horns, etc.
  2. If goods are found to be faulty, damaged or expired, they will be confiscated or thrown away
  3. No refunds will be given after confirmation of purchase of goods unless faulty
  4. Do not use personal details of another student during registration or login
  5. Keep the reciept of sale carefully incase of any future incidences
  6. A user who does not adhere to this rules will have his/her account deactivated, and WILL face punishment according to the school rules and regulations.

Tips

  1. Foodstuffs that are rare in the common market will do well on the platform, for example, unique soda varieties
  2. Always add a clear, visible picture of your commodity to attract buyers
  3. A clear, brief description of your commodity helps to pull buyers
  4. Balance discount given and profit made effectively to avoid making a huge loss
